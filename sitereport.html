Site Report

In these past few weeks while I have been doing my assignment of developing a website. I have faced many problems and it was hard to find the solutions to my problems. But after a long time and process, I have finally completed my website by making it responsive and viewable. While developing this website I got help from my senior and some other site as I have mentioned in the references. I have learned a few things about coding while developing this website and I got a chance to show my creativity and experiences to everyone.
